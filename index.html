About The School

Welcome to Cornerstone Secondary School! We are committed to providing high-quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment for all our students.

Our school offers a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and support services to help students achieve their full potential.

Founded in 2015, Cornerstone Secondary School has a rich history of academic excellence and community engagement in West Nile region . Our dedicated team of educators, staff, and administrators work together to create a positive and inclusive school culture where every student can thrive.
